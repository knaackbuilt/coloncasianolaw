The OGS Bureau of Commercial Management is the primary point of contact for all commercial lease activity in OGS-managed buildings. The Bureau:

  • Procures new tenants;
  • Negotiates and writes lease agreements for state-owned space; and
  • Manages lease agreements.

Parties interested in leasing retail space should respond to Requests for Information posted below, or contact the Bureau of Commercial Management by email at foodservice@ogs.ny.gov.
